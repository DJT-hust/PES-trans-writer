# PES Phrase Bank

Use these as patterns, not fixed sentences.

## Positioning

- The studied problem is challenging because ...
- This limitation becomes pronounced when ...
- Existing approaches usually require ...
- Such an assumption may be restrictive in ...
- This paper focuses on ... rather than ...

## Method bridge

- The key idea is to ...
- The resulting formulation preserves ...
- The reformulation enables ...
- The proposed procedure consists of ...
- The learned component is embedded as ...

## Results

- The reduction is mainly attributed to ...
- The binding constraint indicates that ...
- The comparison isolates the effect of ...
- The result suggests that ... under ...
- This trade-off is caused by ...

## Limitations and future work

- The present model assumes ...
- Extending the framework to ... is left for future work.
- Future work will consider ...

## Phrases to avoid

- It is worth noting that
- In today's rapidly evolving landscape
- Revolutionary / groundbreaking / cutting-edge
- Comprehensive analysis
- Extensive simulations validate the superiority
- The proposed framework paves the way
- Significant improvement without quantified evidence
