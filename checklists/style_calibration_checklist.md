# Style Calibration Checklist

- [ ] The prose follows the completed `narrative_logic_map.md`, not a generic section template.
- [ ] Introduction begins with a concrete power-system or market context, not a generic AI-style opening.
- [ ] Literature review is grouped by technical approach and each group links to the declared gap.
- [ ] Gap statement is specific and not exaggerated.
- [ ] Contributions are concrete, limited to two or three, and directly supported by experiments or derivations.
- [ ] Each result paragraph follows observation, numerical evidence, mechanism, implication, and maps to a contribution.
- [ ] The text avoids unsupported words such as "significant", "superior", "comprehensive", and "novel" unless quantified or justified.
- [ ] Terminology is consistent with IEEE PES usage and the project code.
- [ ] No sentence claims a reference supports something that is not verified in `reference_audit.md`.
